      ShootPhotoMode
      enum ShootPhotoMode
      Package: dji.common.camera
      SDK Key: CameraKey.SHOOT_PHOTO_MODE, CameraKey.SHOOT_PHOTO_MODE_RANGE
      Description:

      The ShootPhoto mode itself can have several modes. The default value is SINGLE.
      ShootPhoto模式本身可以有多种模式。默认值为SINGLE。

      Enum Members:
      SINGLE Sets the camera to take a single photo.
      设置相机以拍摄单个照片。
      HDR Sets the camera to take an HDR photo. X5 camera, X5R camera, XT camera, Z30 camera, Phantom 4 Pro camera, X4S camera and X5S camera do not support HDR mode.
      设置相机以拍摄HDR照片。X5相机、X5R相机、XT相机、Z30相机、Phantom 4 Pro相机、X4S相机和X5S相机不支持HDR模式。
      BURST Set the camera to take multiple photos at once. XT camera does not support Burst mode.
      将相机设置为一次拍摄多张照片。XT相机不支持突发模式。
      AEB Automatic Exposure Bracketing (AEB) capture. In this mode you can quickly take multiple shots (the default is 3) at different exposures without having to manually change any settings between frames. XT camera and Z30 camera does not support AEB mode.
      自动曝光支架（AEB）捕获。在此模式下，您可以在不同的曝光下快速拍摄多张照片（默认值为3张），而无需手动更改帧之间的任何设置。XT相机和Z30相机不支持AEB模式。
      INTERVAL Sets the camera to take a picture (or multiple pictures) continuously at a set time interval. The minimum interval for JPEG format of any quality is 2s. For all cameras except X4S, X5S and Phantom 4 Pro camera: The minimum interval for RAW or RAW+JPEG format is 10s. For the X4S, X5S and Phantom 4 Pro cameras the minimum interval for RAW or RAW+JPEG dformat is 5s.
      将相机设置为以设置的时间间隔连续拍摄一张照片（或多张照片）。任何质量的JPEG格式的最小间隔为2s。对于除X4S、X5S和Phantom 4 Pro相机外的所有相机：RAW或RAW+JPEG格式的最小间隔为10秒。对于X4S、X5S和Phantom 4 Pro相机，RAW或RAW+JPEG数据格式的最小间隔为5秒。
      TIME_LAPSE Sets the camera to take a picture (or multiple pictures) continuously at a set time interval. The camera will merge the photo sequence and the output is a video. The minimum interval for Video only format is 1 s. The minimum interval for Video+Photo format is 2 s. For the new Osmo firmware version, no video feed will be received if the camera is shooting photos with Time-lapse mode. Instead, user can receive a sequence of preview images using the delegate method onGenerate. Supported only by Osmo camera (Z3 and X3).
      将相机设置为以设置的时间间隔连续拍摄一张照片（或多张照片）。相机将合并照片序列，输出为视频。仅视频格式的最小间隔为1秒。视频+照片格式的最小时间间隔为2秒。对于新的Osmo固件版本，如果相机使用延时模式拍摄照片，则不会收到视频馈送。相反，用户可以使用委托方法onGenerate接收一系列预览图像。仅由Osmo相机（Z3和X3）支持。
      PANORAMA In panorama mode, the aircraft takes a series of photos with different gimbal and aircraft heading positions. The photos can be stitched with a third-party library. It is supported by Spark, Mavic Air, Mavic 2, Mavic 2 Pro, Mavic 2 Zoom, Mavic 2 Eneterprise, Mavic 2 Enterprise Dual.
      在全景模式下，飞机用不同的万向节和飞机航向位置拍摄一系列照片。可以使用第三方库缝合照片。它由Spark、Mavic Air、Mavic2、Mavic3 Pro、Mavic4 Zoom、Mavic5 Enterprise和Mavic 2 Enterprise Dual提供支持。
      EHDR Sets the camera to take an enhanced-HDR photo. It blends a sequence of photos for ghost-free high dynamic range photos. It is supported by Mavic 2 Zoom and Mavic 2 Pro.
      设置相机以拍摄增强的HDR照片。它混合了一系列照片，以获得无重影的高动态范围照片。它由Mavic 2 Zoom和Mavic 3 Pro支持。
      HYPER_LIGHT Sets the camera to take photos in HyperLight mode. It is a setting for low-light environment to enhance the photo quality while reducing noise significantly. It is supported by Mavic 2 Zoom and Mavic 2 Pro.
      将相机设置为在HyperLight模式下拍摄照片。这是针对低光环境的设置，以提高照片质量，同时显著降低噪声。它由Mavic 2 Zoom和Mavic 3 Pro支持。
      HIGH_RESOLUTION Indicates that the photo was taken in high resolution mode(48 MP 8000×6000 pixel). Supported by Mavic Air 2.
      表示照片是在高分辨率模式下拍摄的（48 MP 8000×6000像素）。由Mavic Air 2提供支持。
      RAW_BURST Sets the camera to take a burst of RAW photos. Use getRAWPhotoBurstCount to check how many photos have been shot. Only supported by X5S.
      设置相机以拍摄一组RAW照片。使用getRAWPhotoBurstCount检查拍摄了多少张照片。仅受X5S支持。
      SHALLOW_FOCUS Sets the camera to take a series of photos while the aircraft moves up and down automatically. The image's depth of field can be computed with a third-party library to produce a photo with the bokeh effect. Note:
      1. It is only suitable for shooting still scenes.
      2. The aircraft will ascend about 20cm before shooting. Ensure that there is no obstacle about the aircraft.
      3. The suitable range of shooting is within 30m.
      It is only supported by Spark.
      设置相机在飞机自动上下移动时拍摄一系列照片。可以使用第三方库计算图像的景深，以生成具有bokeh效果的照片。注：1。它只适合拍摄静止场景。2.飞机将在射击前上升约20厘米。确保飞机周围没有障碍物。3.适宜的射击距离在30m以内。它仅受Spark支持。
      UNKNOWN The shoot photo mode is unknown.
      拍摄照片模式未知。
